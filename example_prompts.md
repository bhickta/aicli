**CORE PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)**

1.  **Zero Information Loss (Absolute Rule):**
    *   Every specific Entity (Name, Place, Organization), Statistic, Date, Definition, and Technical Term MUST be preserved.
    *   **Do not summarize away details.** Compress the *representation* of the detail, not the detail itself. If a list of 5 items is given, all 5 must be listed.

2.  **Extreme Conciseness (Telegraphic Style):**
    *   **No Grammar:** Eliminate articles (a, an, the), auxiliary verbs (is, are, was, have), and connective phrases.
    *   **Symbolic Logic:** Use symbols to replace words for relationships:
        *   `->` (leads to, causes, results in)
        *   `=` (is, definition, same as)
        *   `!=` (not, different from)
        *   `+` (and, plus, along with)
        *   `vs` (against, contrast, versus)
        *   `^` (increase, high, rise)
        *   `v` (decrease, low, fall)
        *   `~` (approx, related to)
    *   **Merge Details:** Collapse complex sentences into single linear chains of logic.

3.  **Strict Content Filter (No Gossip):**
    *   **Fact over Hearsay:** Focus strictly on governance facts, policy decisions, official statements, and verifiable events.
    *   **No Political Gossip:** Aggressively filter out speculation, personal attacks, "political angle," backstage gossip, or subjective opinions regarding political figures. If a statement is purely opinion or rumor without factual basis, exclude it.
